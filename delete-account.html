StageReturn – Account Deletion Request

This page is intended to comply with Google Play’s account deletion policy.

If you wish to delete your StageReturn account and associated data, please contact us at:

support@stagereturn.com

Please include in your email:

  • The email address associated with your account
  • The name of your company (if applicable)

What will be deleted:

  • User account information (email, display name)
  • Company membership data
  • Project and equipment data associated with your account
  • Uploaded photos

Data retention:

Billing records may be retained if required by law.

Account deletion requests are processed within 30 days.
